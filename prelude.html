One object.
Three incompatible readings.
Institutional
"Displayed within the machinery gallery of an oval palace."
Omits: what the machine is built to do.
Staged
"Part of a triumphal spectacle — music, bells, detonations."
Omits: the violence embedded in the object itself.
Personal
"Imposing. Rewarded. A machine that draws admiration."
"These engines are made to kill."
The contradiction is not an error. It is the archive.
